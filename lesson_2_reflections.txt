Breaking!

Academy-Award winner Dean Ayer (Batman: Knightfall, The Imitation Game)

has signed on to direct 'A Monster Calls' for Focus Pictures

from a script by Academy-Award winner Max Landis!


Felicity Jones is expected to star, reuniting Ayer and Jones
for the first time since starring together in 'Batman: Knightfall' and
more famously in 'Batman: Hush' and 'Midnight in Paris.'
